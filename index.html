Teller lets you check your bank account using your Amazon Echo

With Teller, checking your bank account is as easy as asking. Simply ask Teller what your balance, how much money you have, or what your net worth is, and Teller will return your current bank account balance.

Get started with Teller

  1. Go into your Amazon Alexa App

  2. Select the Menu

  3. Select "Skills"

  4. Search for "Teller"

  5. Click Enable 👏👏👏
